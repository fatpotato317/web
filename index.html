About $SOLDAY

$SOLDAY is the ultimate memecoin tribute to Solana's birthday! Born to celebrate the speed, power, and community of Solana, $SOLDAY is more than just a token—it's a yearly crypto party! Every year on Solana's birthday, we bring airdrops, events, and celebrations to the Solana ecosystem. No team allocations, no VC funding—just a fun, community-driven coin honoring the blockchain we love. Join us, grab some $SOLDAY, and let's make Solana's birthday the biggest party in crypto!
